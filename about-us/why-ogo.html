Why OGO (ORANGE GLOBAL OLYMPIADS) ?


OGO (ORANGE GLOBAL OLYMPIADS) is a precisely planned skill based examination to test the achievement levels of students and encourage them to achieve higher learning benchmarks.


  • Multiple choice questions, are based on Bloom’s Taxonomy, evaluates the cognitive skill levels of students in order to promote a deeper level of complex learning.
  • Elevated benchmark of exam conducted in 2 levels i.e School Level Examination (SLE) or National Level Examination (NLE)
  • Examines the basic understanding of the concept and provides detailed reports on the student’s performance against peers all over the country
  • OGO (ORANGE GLOBAL OLYMPIADS) helps students discover and reach their optimum potential
  • OGO (ORANGE GLOBAL OLYMPIADS) develops creative & critical thinking and problem solving analysis (PSA)
  • Rather than testing rote learning, OGO (ORANGE GLOBAL OLYMPIADS) measures the extent to which the concepts and skills underlying the school syllabus have been understood by students and provides a comparative analysis.
